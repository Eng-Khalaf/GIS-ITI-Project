About Project

In 5 Minutes, Find Your Desired Nighboorhood For Your Study or Work


Every Semester Students Change thier Accommodations due to many reasons eg: the lack of safety or services, unfortunately this happenes alot because "as Expatriate Students / Workers" we don't have the required knowledge. Actually we faced that exact problem when we went to Qena for the first time to study a whole month for our (ITI GIS track), Although we have a helpfull friend who helped us alot in searching, but we found some nighbourhoods are expensive & far from Creativa Building! & some are cheap & near but lack of safety & services!, And Count on & on...!
that experience drove us to Fix the problem from it's Roots!
Simbly suggesting some exact nighbourhoods rather than others.
